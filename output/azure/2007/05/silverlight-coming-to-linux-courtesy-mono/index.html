Silverlight Coming to Linux Courtesy Mono

According to an article over at news.com, the Mono project founder says they’ll be working on a Linux port of Silverlight in the future. Sweet! I was personally annoyed to find out that “cross-platform” only meant Mac and Windows when Silverlight was officially announced. But it’s nice to see Mono committing to filling the Linux gap. Best of luck to them!
