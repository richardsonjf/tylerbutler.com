C’mon iPhone, be serious…

Warren Spector on the iPhone:

It’s like the iPhone is working so hard to be my friend it’s incapable of being my co-worker. It’s all fun and games when, at times, I want it to be serious.

Good points all around. If you want to use your phone for doc editing, or come from a Palm/Windows Mobile background and are used to rich syncing with the desktop, the iPhone is lacking.

More on who Spector is, for those that don’t know. Deus Ex remains one of my favorite games of all time.
